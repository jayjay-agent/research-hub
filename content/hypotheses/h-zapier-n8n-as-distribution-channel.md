## Hypothesis

**If** Agent Studio publishes Algolia agent capabilities as native Zapier and n8n steps (search-and-rank, rule-aware retrieval, strict-rule enforcement, analytics insight),
**then** we'll see meaningful net-new account creation from Zapier/n8n marketplace discovery within 90 days of launch,
**because** the dominant builder population already lives in those runtimes and a majority used them as the primary surface for agentic workflows.

## How to test

- Ship MVP integration with 3-5 high-value Algolia primitives as Zapier and n8n steps.
- Track: install count, accounts created via marketplace discovery, retention at 30 days.
- Compare to organic Agent Studio signup velocity over the same period.

## Risks to the hypothesis

- Confidence is 3 because "meeting builders where they are" is a common assumption that doesn't always cash out — marketplaces are noisy and discovery is hard.
- Zapier/n8n take pricing and rate-limit cuts that may compress our economics; viability of this channel depends on what we put behind the integration.
- May cannibalize direct-Agent-Studio signups if the same value is available with less friction in Zapier.
- Builder retention from this channel may be worse than direct because they're shallower users.
