## Task

Track the wide-scale survey that the Research & Insights team is planning as the follow-up to this qualitative phase. When it lands:

- Re-evaluate which dream AI products have meaningful tail support beyond the 10-customer sample.
- Specifically look for whether smaller / non-eCommerce customers also prefer orchestratable primitives over turnkey products — current sample skews enterprise eCommerce.
- Re-rank the findings by quantitative confidence and update the implication docs.

## Why now

The qualitative findings are sharp, but a 10-customer sample can't tell us whether the orchestration-over-monolith preference holds at scale. Several hypotheses (especially [[h-orchestratable-primitives-beat-monolithic-suite]]) need that data before we'd commit roadmap-level investment.

## Done when

Survey is published, ingested as a new source via `/research-strategy`, and a delta-summary is written explaining what changed in our confidence levels.
