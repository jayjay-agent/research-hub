Research → Strategy, without the leap of faith.

How I built a workspace that refuses to display ungrounded strategy — and how you could, too.

01 · The problem

Strategy and research live in different orbits.

Customer interviews live in Notion. PDFs sit on Drive. Quotes scatter across Slack threads. By week three, "we heard from users that…" becomes folklore — and no one can point back to the source.

For Agent Studio specifically: if I can't trace a claim, I can't defend the bet I'm making on top of it.

04 · The workflow

/research-strategy, end-to-end.

01 Paste a URL — /research-strategy https://… → ingest
02 Skill writes source.md — full text plus metadata → sources/
03 Generates summary, findings (each with a verbatim quote), implications → findings/ + summaries/
04 Drafts hypotheses, ideas, design goals — only if they can cite → hypotheses/
05 Reports back: counts, sharpest implications, and ideas it refused to commit ↩ THE MAGIC MOMENT
RESEARCH → STRATEGY 05 / 10
05 · The most important slide

The tool refuses to lie.

If an idea can't cite a source, the skill won't write it to disk. It surfaces it as ungrounded — so I can decide whether to investigate, not whether to remember.

This is the part that keeps the workspace honest at month three, month six, month twelve.

06 · Three decisions worth calling out

Constraints that earn their keep.

01 STORAGE

Markdown-first. No database.

Content is the source of truth. The app is just a reader. Portable, diffable, grep-able, AI-legible — and survives if I never touch the reader again.

→ content/ on disk
02 STRUCTURE

Wikilinks over folder hierarchy.

Knowledge graphs beat trees for research. [[h-eval-determinism]] resolves anywhere — no path memorisation, no broken links on rename.

→ src/lib/wikilinks.ts
03 SYSTEM

Built on Satellite.

Reused our own design system end-to-end. The meta-point: a tool for Algolia thinkers should look like an Algolia surface. Plus, free dark mode.

→ @algolia/satellite

09 · What's next

Where I'd take it next.

NEXT Confidence decay. Hypotheses age. A bet from research six months old should look different from one made last week — surface that visually.
SOON Cross-project citation graph. If two strategy docs both cite the same finding, link them. Compounding becomes legible across teams, not just within one workspace.
EXPLORING Skill packaging. Make /research-strategy portable — drop it into any markdown repo, not just this one.
LATER Designer-facing capture. Surface ungrounded ideas as a worklist designers can claim — turn refusal into next-research-question.
